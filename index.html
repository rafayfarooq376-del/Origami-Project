Origami For Everyone

Origami is the Japanese art of paper folding, derived from the words "ori" (to fold) and "kami" (paper). It involves transforming a flat sheet of paper, most often square, into a finished sculpture through folding and sculpting techniques, creating a wide range of subjects like animals, flowers, and geometric shapes. Beyond a creative hobby, origami is used as a tool for education and can improve skills like concentration, problem-solving, and hand-eye coordination.
